The Challenge

The organization's audit function relied on disconnected spreadsheets, shared drives, and email threads to manage engagements across internal audit, compliance review, and external coordinator teams. Planning cycles were slow, evidence was difficult to trace, and leadership lacked real-time visibility into open findings and remediation status.

With increasing regulatory scrutiny and a growing audit portfolio, the client needed a purpose-built platform to standardize workflows, enforce governance, and shorten the path from audit planning through to committee-ready reporting.

Our Approach

Deep Hill designed and built the Audit Management Platform end to end — working closely with audit leads, risk officers, and IT stakeholders to translate real operating procedures into a scalable digital workflow. The platform was delivered in iterative releases so teams could adopt core capabilities early while advanced features rolled out in parallel.

  • Centralized audit universe and annual planning with risk-based prioritization
  • Engagement workspaces for fieldwork, testing, evidence upload, and reviewer sign-off
  • Findings and action tracking with owners, due dates, and escalation rules
  • Configurable approval workflows and immutable audit trail for every change
  • Executive dashboards for open findings, overdue actions, and engagement progress
  • Role-based access for auditors, business owners, compliance, and leadership

Platform Capabilities

The delivered solution provides a cohesive environment for the full audit lifecycle:

  • Plan — define scope, allocate resources, and schedule engagements from a single audit calendar
  • Execute — run checklists, capture workpapers, and attach evidence with version control
  • Report — generate structured findings with severity, root cause, and management responses
  • Remediate — track corrective actions through validation and closure with automated reminders
  • Assure — provide audit committee and leadership views without manual consolidation

Estimated Benefits

Based on post-implementation analysis and client feedback from the recent rollout, Deep Hill estimates the following percentage improvements:

52% Faster audit cycle completion
48% Reduction in manual evidence collation
42% Improvement in finding closure tracking
38% Faster audit committee reporting turnaround

Estimated benefits are based on observed project outcomes and client-reported improvements from the recent platform rollout. Results vary by organization, scope, and operating environment. No financial figures are quoted.

Outcome

The Audit Management Platform is now the client's system of record for audit operations. Teams spend less time chasing documents and more time on substantive review, while leadership benefits from consistent, timely visibility across the audit portfolio. The architecture is built to extend into integrated risk, compliance, and regulatory reporting workflows as the program matures.
