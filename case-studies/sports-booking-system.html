A multi-city sports management company needed a single system to coordinate venues, events, and staff schedules across locations — replacing fragmented tools and manual coordination.

The Challenge

Operations spanned multiple cities, each running its own booking processes through spreadsheets, email chains, and disconnected calendar tools. Venue double-bookings, last-minute schedule changes, and poor visibility into staff availability created friction for both internal teams and customers.

The company needed a purpose-built platform that could handle complex scheduling rules, multi-venue availability, recurring events, and role-based access for coordinators, coaches, and administrators.

Our Approach

Deep Hill designed and built a custom booking and schedule management application tailored to the company's operating model. We conducted workflow workshops in each city, prototyped key user journeys, and delivered an MVP before expanding functionality based on real usage patterns.

  • Centralized venue and resource inventory across all cities
  • Real-time availability search with conflict detection
  • Automated notifications for schedule changes and approvals
  • Role-based dashboards for admins, coordinators, and staff
  • Reporting on utilization, bookings, and operational bottlenecks

Estimated Benefits

Based on post-implementation analysis and client feedback, Deep Hill estimates the following percentage improvements:

60% Reduction in scheduling conflicts
50% Faster venue booking turnaround
40% Improvement in staff utilization across cities
35% Increase in booking channel efficiency

Estimated benefits are based on observed project outcomes and client-reported improvements. Results vary by organization, scope, and operating environment. No financial figures are quoted.

Outcome

The new system became the operational backbone for the company's multi-city network. Teams gained a shared source of truth for schedules and bookings, while leadership received visibility into capacity and performance across locations — enabling smarter planning and faster customer response.
